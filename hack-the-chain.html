HACK THE CHAIN
A Seismic Privacy Game

You are a hacker. Your mission: steal data from blockchain transactions.

Public chains? Easy. Everything is exposed.
But when you hit a Seismic-encrypted chain… good luck. 🔒

Powered by Seismic · seismic.systems

🎮 How to Play

1
You'll see a blockchain transaction — like a hacker intercepting live chain data.
2
Answer questions about the transaction. On public chains, data is visible and easy to steal.
3
On Seismic chains, data is encrypted — you'll get blocked every time you try to steal it.
4
You have 3 lives ❤️ and 15 seconds per question. Score points and learn why privacy matters!
